FAQ

Questions & answers

Will the AI change my architecture or floor plan? +
No. The AI only enhances materials, lighting, textures and photorealism. The geometry, walls, openings, furniture positions and room proportions remain exactly as in your original render.
What render styles does it produce? +
The AI produces Scandinavian / Japandi style photorealistic renders — warm oak materials, soft daylight, marble-look tiles, neutral tones. Ideal for residential and high-end commercial projects.
What file formats are accepted? +
JPG and PNG files up to 20MB. For best results, upload renders at minimum 1024×768 pixels.
Is there a subscription or minimum spend? +
No subscription, no minimum. You pay €0.20 per render, only when you're happy with the preview. No account required.
Is my render data kept private? +
Your uploaded renders are used only to generate the enhanced version and are not stored, shared or used to train any AI models.
